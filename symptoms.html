How do I know if my computer have a virus?
It may not be immediately obvious when your computer has a virus. Take a look at the table below and see if you computer has one or more of the following symptoms. If so, your computer may be infected.

Symptoms Description
Hardware Troubles- Hardware failing to perform or respond. Printers, Mice, keyboards, etc.
No Response- Locking up, freezing, or crashing
Slow Performance- Taking longer than usual to perform tasks
Slow Startup- Booting up now takes a lot longer time
File Troubles- Files are moved, deleted or not accessible
Extra files- Extra files appearing out of nowhere
Drives- Disc or harddrives not responding
Slow Performance- Taking longer than usual to perform tasks
Unusual Error Messages- Error messages containing gibberish or unusual characters
