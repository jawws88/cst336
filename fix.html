How do I fix my computer once I know it's been infected
Ok now that you know your computer has been infected it's time to take action. Here is a brief step by step guide on different ways to remove any virus.
1 Update the computer to the latest version and make sure any antivirus that is installed is up to date.
2 Erradicate the virus with a good virus removal software, then do a full scan to ensure you removed all of the virus.
3 Quarantine any piece of virus that is unable to be removed. Then backup any files that you need to keep.
4 Reinstall, perform a clean install of the operating system.
