What is a Computer Virus?
A computer virus is similar to a bioligical virus. For example to when you catch a stomach virus and end up in the bathroom all day... It's the same when a computer gets a virus instead of being stuck in the bathroom the computer can be acting slow or dumping data. Even the most tech savy are susceptible to getting a virus.


How do you get a computer virus?
The web can be a dangerous place and a virus can be accidentally downloaded; by installing a free game, going to a untrusted website, opening attachments, installing a free app. One doesn't have to be online to catch a virus. The virus could be installed by installing a program from a disc.
